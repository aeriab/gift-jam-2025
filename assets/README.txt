//HOW TO USE//

Each tile is 8x8 pixels

first row: contains all the player animations
	idle | 1 sprite
	walk | 4 sprites
	going up stairs | 2 sprites
	damaged | 1 sprite

enemys animations
	2 walk sprites + 1 damaged sprite

water and waterfall
	2 animation sprites

coin rotating
	4 animation sprites
